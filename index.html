why must healthy food?

We depend on certain foods to provide us with nutrients, vitamins, and minerals the body needs to stay healthy. Without these vitamins and nutrients, we feel the effects, which oftentimes includes lack of energy.

The foods you eat play a role in keeping your brain healthy and can improve specific mental tasks, such as memory and concentration.

Feeding your body certain foods may help keep your immune system strong. The immune system relies on white blood cells that produce antibodies to combat bacteria, viruses, and other invaders.

Food helps reduce your risk of developing some chronic diseases. New research finds that your food choices may also affect your mood and mental health. This is sometimes called the "food-mood connection"
